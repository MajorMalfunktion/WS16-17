\\
$SkolemForm:\\$
\varphi' =  \exists y[\neg P(y) \lor \forall z \neg Q(z,f(y))] \land \forall z[\exists y Q(y,f(z)) \land P(z)]\\
\equiv      [\neg P(c) \lor \forall z \neg Q(z,f(c))] \land \forall z[Q(g(z),f(z)) \land P(z)]\\

= \varphi''\\
